Core Standards You NEED for a Multi-Agent System

1️⃣ Functional Safety & Cybersecurity
These govern safety, hazard analysis, safety requirements, and lifecycle processes:
Mandatory
ISO 26262 – Functional Safety
ISO 21434 – Cybersecurity Engineering

2️⃣ System & Software Lifecycle (Requirements + Development)
These align with your SYS.1–SYS.5 and SWE.x agents:
Highly Recommended
ISO/IEC/IEEE 29148 – Requirements Engineering
ISO/IEC/IEEE 15288 – System Lifecycle Processes
ISO/IEC/IEEE 12207 – Software Lifecycle Processes
IREB CPRE Guidelines – Requirement Engineering Best Practice
INCOSE SE Handbook – System Engineering Framework

3️⃣ Diagnostics & OBD Standards
For your Diagnostics Agent + RAG ingestion:
ISO 14229 – UDS (Unified Diagnostic Services)
ISO 15031 – OBD-II Diagnostics
SAE J1979 – OBD PIDs
SAE J2012 – Diagnostic Trouble Codes

4️⃣ Communication Standards (CAN, LIN, FlexRay, Ethernet)
For Communication Agent + message/signal requirements:
Mandatory
ISO 11898 – CAN Standard
ISO 17987 – LIN
ISO 17458 – FlexRay
IEEE 802.3 Automotive Ethernet
SOME/IP (Autosar) Specification

5️⃣ Environmental, Durability & Electrical Tests
For Component Agent & Validation Agent:
ISO 16750 – Environmental & Electrical Tests
LV124 / LV148 (VDA) – German OEM Low Voltage Tests
IEC 60068 – Environmental Tests
ISO 7637 – Electrical Disturbances in Vehicles
ISO 16735 – EMC for vehicle components
(You already have ISO 16750 + LV_VDA which covers most practical needs.)

6️⃣ EV-Specific High Voltage Safety
For HV Battery Agent, EV Powertrain Agent:
ECE R100 – High Voltage Safety for EVs
ISO 6469 – EV Functional Safety
ISO 21780 – LV DC Distribution
(You already have ECE R100.)

7️⃣ Software Architecture & Autosar
If your Multi-Agent System needs to generate autosar-compliant requirements:
AUTOSAR Classic Platform Specs
AUTOSAR Adaptive Platform Specs
AUTOSAR CP SWC Templates

8️⃣ General Vehicle Standards (optional but useful)
ISO 21448 (SOTIF) – Safety of Intended Functionality (for ADAS/AI)
ISO 14300 – Project Management & Risk in Automotive
UNECE Regulations (various) – If required by market
